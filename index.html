About Me

Katie Waters is a dedicated professional with a passion for technology and a diverse background in Healthcare and Computer Science. Katie's adaptable nature, coupled with her strong work ethic and problem-solving abilities, has consistently advanced her in every endeavor. Her healthcare background has instilled in her a deep sense of empathy and a client-focused mindset, which she integrates into her work as a web developer. Katie approaches every project with precision and creativity, striving to create innovative and user-friendly solutions. With her diverse skill set, multilingual proficiency, and determination, Katie Waters is poised to make a meaningful impact in the world of technology and wellness. She seeks opportunities to collaborate with like-minded professionals, contribute to cutting-edge projects, and continue her educational journey, all while staying at the forefront of the ever-evolving fields of computer science and holistic well-being.
